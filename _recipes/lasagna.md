---
layout: recipe
title: Lasagna
prep_time: 2 hours
cook_time: 50 minutes
temperature: 375°F
servings: 12-15
source: Freda Lapp
category: Dinner
protein: beef

ingredients: |
  **Meat Sauce**
  - 2 lbs ground beef
  - Three 8 oz cans tomato sauce
  - Two 6 oz cans tomato paste
  - 2 cloves garlic, minced
  - 2 tsp sugar
  - 1 tsp italian seasoning
  - 1 tsp salt
  - 1/2 tsp pepper

  **Cheese Mix**
  - 3 large eggs, beaten
  - 3 tsp dried parsley
  - 24 oz cottage cheese
  - 8 oz ricotta cheese
  - 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese

  **Other ingredients**
  - 9 lasagna noodles, cooked and drained
  - 6 slices provolone cheese
  - 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  - 
  **Half Meat Sauce**
  - 1 lb ground beef
  - 12 oz tomato sauce
  - 6 oz tomato paste
  - 1 cloves garlic, minced
  - 1 tsp sugar
  - 1/2 tsp italian seasoning
  - 1/2 tsp salt
  - 1/4 tsp pepper

  **Half Cheese Mix**
  - 2 large eggs, beaten
  - 1.5 tsp dried parsley
  - 12 oz cottage cheese
  - 4 oz ricotta cheese
  - 1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese

  **Half Other ingredients**
  - 6 lasagna noodles, cooked and drained
  - 3 slices provolone cheese
  - 1.5 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

instructions: |
  1. In a large skillet, cook beef until no longer pink; drain. Add remaining meat sauce ingredients. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat; simmer uncovered for 1 hour, stirring occasionally.
  2. In a large bowl, combine cheese mix ingredients.
  3. Preheat oven to 375°F.
  4. For full recipe, using a 9x13 ungreased baking dish, layer in the following order:
     - 1 cup meat sauce
     - 3 noodles
     - all provolone slices
     - 2 cups cheese mix
     - 1 cup mozzarella
     - 3 noodles
     - 2 cups meat sauce
     - remaining cheese mix
     - 1 cup mozzarella
     - remaining noodles, meat sauce, mozzarella
  5. For half recipe, using a 9x9 ungreased baking dish, layer in the following order:
     - 1/2 cup meat sauce
     - 2 noodles
     - all provolone slices
     - 1 cup cheese mix
     - 1/2 cup mozzarella
     - 2 noodles
     - 1 cup meat sauce
     - remaining cheese mix
     - 1/2 cup mozzarella
     - remaining noodles, meat sauce, mozzarella

notes: |
  - Serve alongside salad with [italian dressing](/recipes/recipes/dressing-italian/) and [garlic bread](/recipes/recipes/bread-garlic/).
